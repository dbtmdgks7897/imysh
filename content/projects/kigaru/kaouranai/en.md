## Background — WHY

The first service on the kigaru platform, providing fun face-reading analysis from a single photo.
"kaouranai" is a portmanteau of the Korean word for face reading (관상) and the Japanese "どうですか (how is it?)."

## Features

- Photo upload and AI face reading analysis
- Results displayed as personality, fortune, and trait cards
- SNS-shareable result image

## Technology

Leverages Gemini Flash's Vision capabilities to analyze facial images,
using structured prompts to generate consistent results.
